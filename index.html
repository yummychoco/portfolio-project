My Skills

Dancer

As a dancer, you'll use movement, gesture and body language to portray a character, story, situation or abstract concept to an audience, usually to the accompaniment of music.

Choreographer

As a choreographer, you'll create and plan routines for performance, usually to music or to evoke an idea in the heads of the audience. You'll usually required to plan the whole performance, from teaching steps to the dancers to working with costume designers and directors to achieve the desired outcome.
